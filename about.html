ABOUT

RSE Labs first started as a work improvement exercise for changing the way vibration data logging is done. As we all know it's tedious !


After conducting a large amount of research, we discovered that the tools we learnt can do much more than just vibration measurement. Our study of advanced micrcontrollers such as STM32 ARM and high speed prototyping tools such as ARDUINO can enable us to develop reliability monitoring systems!

We leverage a strong ecosystem:-

1. We work near the railway depots which can provide the necessary place to test our gadgets!

2. We live next to ShenZhen which is a convenient hub for manufacturing!

RSE Labs team consists of mechanical engineers from T&ESD equip  with microelectronics, manufacturing, mechanical design, and simulation knowledge.
